Research Grant Analysis

Uses a data set of RePORTER Publications in 2023 from the National Institutes of Health with ~84,000 studies to determine whether or not there's a correlation between the population of a city and the cost to do a study there as well as filteres through each abstract to find the most frequently used words in them. This project was created for and presented to the Michigan Data Science Team.
